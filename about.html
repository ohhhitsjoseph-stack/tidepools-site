Our Story

About Oceara

Making environmental DNA analysis accessible to everyone who protects our waters.

Our Mission

We believe biodiversity monitoring shouldn't require a PhD in bioinformatics. Oceara transforms complex eDNA sequencing data into clear, actionable insights that conservation teams, coastal agencies, and researchers can use immediately.

The Problem We Solve

Traditional eDNA analysis requires specialized expertise, expensive software, and weeks of processing time. Most environmental teams lack the resources to turn raw sequencing data into meaningful conservation decisions.

Our Approach

Oceara combines automated bioinformatics pipelines with AI-powered interpretation to deliver stakeholder-ready reports in minutes, not weeks. No coding required. No bioinformatics degree needed.

Who We Serve

  • Conservation organizations monitoring endangered species
  • Coastal agencies tracking invasive species
  • Research teams studying aquatic biodiversity
  • Environmental consultants conducting assessments
