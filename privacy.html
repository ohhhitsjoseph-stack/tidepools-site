1. Overview

Oceara is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our eDNA bioinformatics platform, including our website, mobile applications, and related services.

Oceara provides a comprehensive platform for environmental DNA (eDNA) analysis, enabling research teams, marine agencies, and conservation organizations to monitor biodiversity, track species populations, and support data-driven ocean conservation decisions. Our platform processes sensitive biological and genomic data, and we take your privacy seriously.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information in connection with the services we provide:

Account Details

When you create an Oceara account, we collect your name, email address, organization/institution, role, and password. We may also collect your phone number and billing address if you subscribe to a paid plan.

Sample Metadata

You provide metadata about your eDNA samples, including collection location, collection date, sample type, environmental conditions, species target information, and project-specific annotations or notes.

Raw Sequencing Files

We store raw sequencing data files (FASTQ format) that you upload to our platform. These files contain the genetic sequences obtained from your eDNA samples.

Analysis Outputs

Our platform generates and stores analysis results, including species identification data, abundance estimates, quality metrics, phylogenetic assignments, and custom report outputs.

Usage Data

We automatically collect information about your interactions with the platform, including:

  • Log data (IP address, browser type, access times, pages viewed)
  • Device information (operating system, device type, unique identifiers)
  • Platform analytics (features used, analyses performed, data processed)
  • Performance metrics and system diagnostics

Billing Details

If you use our paid services, we collect billing information including payment method details, subscription tier, and transaction history. Payment processing is handled by PCI-DSS compliant third-party processors.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes:

Platform Operation

To provide, maintain, and improve the Oceara eDNA bioinformatics platform, including processing your samples, running analyses, storing results, and managing your account.

Report Generation

To generate customized analysis reports, visualizations, and data summaries based on your input data and selected analysis parameters.

Communication

To send you service-related announcements, technical updates, security alerts, and respond to your inquiries. We may also send product updates and feature notifications if you have opted in.

Platform Improvement

To analyze usage patterns, understand user needs, optimize platform performance, and develop new features and services. This may include aggregated and anonymized analytics.

Fraud Detection

To detect, prevent, and address fraudulent activities, unauthorized access, and other security incidents.

Legal Compliance

To comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, and government requests. We will disclose information as required by law or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary.

4. How We Share Information

We may share your information with selected third parties to provide and improve our services:

Cloud Infrastructure

We use Supabase for data storage, authentication, and database management. Your data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure with appropriate safeguards.

Compute Providers

We utilize Modal for scalable computational analysis of your eDNA data. Sequencing data and analysis requests are processed through Modal's infrastructure.

AI Providers

We integrate Anthropic's AI services for enhanced analysis capabilities, including data interpretation and report generation. We only transmit data necessary for these specific functions, and Anthropic does not use your eDNA data for model training.

Authorized Collaborators

With your explicit permission, we allow you to share projects, samples, and results with team members and collaborators. You control which data is shared and with whom.

Legal Authorities

We may disclose information when required by law, court order, subpoena, or when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

Service Providers

We may share non-sensitive information with vendors who assist with billing, customer support, analytics, and other business operations. These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You maintain control over your data and can request export or deletion at any time.

Active Accounts

While your Oceara account is active, we retain all your sample data, analysis results, metadata, and account information to provide continuous access to your projects and historical analyses.

Data Export

You can export your data at any time in standard formats (FASTQ, CSV, JSON) for your own records or use with other bioinformatics tools.

Account Deletion

Upon request, we will delete your account and all associated data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain information by law.

Backup Retention

We may retain backups of your data for up to 90 days after deletion for disaster recovery and data integrity purposes, but these backups are not accessible or searchable.

6. Security

Oceara implements comprehensive technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Technical Safeguards

  • End-to-end encryption for data in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher
  • Encryption at rest for stored data using AES-256
  • Secure authentication with password hashing and optional multi-factor authentication (MFA)
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Intrusion detection and prevention systems

Access Controls

  • Role-based access control limiting employee access to customer data
  • Unique credentials and activity logging for all personnel
  • Principle of least privilege for service access
  • Regular security training for team members

Data Security

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no system is completely secure. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your password and account credentials.

7. Your Choices

You have several options regarding your personal information and data:

Access and Correction

You can access, review, and update your account information and sample metadata directly through the Oceara platform dashboard at any time.

Data Export

You can export your samples, analysis results, and metadata in standard, machine-readable formats for use with other tools or for archival purposes.

Sample Deletion

You can delete individual samples or entire projects from your account. Deleted data will be removed from active systems within 7 days.

Account Deletion

You can request complete deletion of your account and all associated data. Submit this request through our privacy contact below, and we will process it within 30 days.

Communication Preferences

You can opt out of promotional communications and marketing emails through your account settings or by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email. Service-related notifications cannot be opted out of while your account is active.

Cookies and Tracking

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may limit platform functionality.

8. International Transfers

Oceara operates globally, and your information may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws different from your home country.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, your personal data will be protected by appropriate safeguards including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and adequacy decisions where applicable. By using Oceara, you consent to the transfer of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

9. Children's Privacy

Oceara is not intended for or directed to children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 16, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account.

Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided information to Oceara should contact us immediately at privacy@oceara.io.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@oceara.io
  • Support: hello@oceara.io
  • Web: oceara.io

We will respond to your inquiry within 14 business days. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on our website and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of Oceara constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
