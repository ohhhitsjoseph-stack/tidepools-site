# Oceara Brand Identity & Design System

## Brand Overview

**Brand Name:** Oceara
**Domain:** oceara.io
**Tagline:** "The New Era of Environmental DNA"
**Industry:** eDNA Bioinformatics / Conservation Technology

---

## Brand Story

Oceara represents a new era (era) in ocean (ocean) and aquatic ecosystem monitoring. The name evokes the vastness of the ocean while suggesting forward-thinking innovation. Just as the ocean connects all ecosystems, Oceara connects environmental DNA data to actionable biodiversity insights.

### Brand Personality
- **Scientific** — Rigorous, data-driven, trustworthy
- **Accessible** — No-code, approachable, human-centered
- **Forward-thinking** — Innovative, modern, leading-edge
- **Conservation-minded** — Purpose-driven, environmentally conscious
- **Clear** — Transparent, explainable, stakeholder-ready

### Brand Voice
- Professional but not stuffy
- Technical but accessible
- Confident but not arrogant
- Warm but authoritative

## Typography

### Primary Font: Inter
- Clean, modern sans-serif
- Excellent readability at all sizes
- Professional yet approachable

**Weights:**
- 400 Regular — Body text
- 500 Medium — Subheadings, navigation
- 600 SemiBold — Buttons, emphasis
- 700 Bold — Headings

### Secondary Font: Source Serif 4
- For editorial content, quotes, accent text
- Adds warmth and scientific credibility

### Font Hierarchy

| Element | Font | Weight | Size | Line Height |
|---------|------|--------|------|-------------|
| H1 | Inter | 700 | 48-64px | 1.1 |
| H2 | Inter | 700 | 36-40px | 1.2 |
| H3 | Inter | 600 | 24-28px | 1.3 |
| Body | Inter | 400 | 16-18px | 1.6 |
| Caption | Inter | 400 | 14px | 1.5 |
| Button | Inter | 600 | 15px | 1 |

## Logo Usage

### Wordmark
"Oceara" in Inter Bold with custom letter spacing (+0.5px)

### Logo Mark Concept
- Abstract wave/DNA helix fusion
- Circular form suggesting completeness and cycles
- Gradient from Ocean Blue to Seafoam

### Clear Space
Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the "O" in Oceara on all sides.

### Minimum Size
- Digital: 80px width minimum
- Print: 0.75" width minimum

## Competitive Differentiation

Unlike competitors (NatureMetrics, eDNAtec, EnviroDNA), Oceara emphasizes:

1. **No-code accessibility** — Not just for bioinformaticians
2. **Stakeholder-ready outputs** — Reports anyone can understand
3. **Modern design** — Clean, professional, trustworthy
4. **Speed** — "In minutes" messaging
5. **Conservation focus** — Built for those who protect ecosystems
